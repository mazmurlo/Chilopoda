WikiChilopoda

WikiChilopoda is a wiki designed for the sole purpose of documenting centipedes. A specific focus is given on their taxonomy, such as their ranks or descriptions, but other general notes on their biology, from individual species to general morphology are included. As an open freely editable wiki, it's reliant on its editors for its content, enabling its growth and maintenance.
