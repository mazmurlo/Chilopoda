WikiChilopoda

WikiChilopoda is a wiki designed for the sole purpose of documenting centipedes. A specific focus is given on their taxonomy, but broader notes on biology, morphology, and behavior are included. As an open, freely editable wiki, it relies on its editors for growth and maintenance.
